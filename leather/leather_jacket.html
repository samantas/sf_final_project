Massimo Dutti is a clothes manufacturing company that is part of the Inditex group, founded in 1985. A well-known sister brand of Zara.

Should you buy?

In the end, it's up to you! If you are concerned about animal treatment, you might want to consider a denim jacket. If you are concerned about fast fashion practices, opt for a smaller, more local brands and avoid Inditex brands.
